ABOUT US

World’s best augmented reality solution for field operations

We visualize, modify and measure geospatial data with centimeter accuracy in the
field by using GNSS/RTK-integrated augmented reality glasses. Combined with
work instructions and remote assistance, we provide a powerful tool for utilities,
contractors and railways to further digitize and optimize their field operations.

Our Vision

Use hands-free, high precision augmented reality to bring geospatial data to the field in an intelligent, accurate and innovative way helping our customers to revolutionize and optimize their operations in the field.

Our Mission

By providing high precision augmented reality, we simplify work for surveyors, contractors and engineers, allowing operators to execute their work more efficiently while improving data quality and reducing safety and financial risks.

OUR INDUSTRIES

UTILITIES

Empower technical workforce & improve data quality

Due to climate change, utilities are required to invest heavily in their infrastructures. Unfortunately, due to the shortage of a technical workforce, utilities struggle to realize their operational and climate goals. V-Labs specializes in accurate, hands-free visualization and acquisition of geospatial data combined with work instructions and remote assistance. This enables utilities to further digitize and optimize their field operations. As a result, V-Labs helps the industry to achieve their operational goals and reduce its CO2 emissions to protect our precious climate.

CONSTRUCTION

Optimize construction & avoid costly errors

Contractors are under more and more pressure to deliver their projects timely, with high quality and within the acceptable safety standards. This is not an easy task and requires contractors to further digitize field work and optimize performance. To support the construction sector, V-Labs has developed a hands-free, high precision Augmented Reality solution for geospatial data, specialized for the construction industry. It allows engineers to bring their 3D models to the construction site with cm/inch accuracy and avoid errors in design and construction, while simplifying communication with field operators and reducing safety risks.
