V-Labs wins MyGalileoSolutions award from the European GNSS Agency

European GNSS Agency

V-Labs is thrilled announce that we were awarded the second prize of the prestigious MyGalileoSolution – Track 2 contest organized by the European GNSS Agency, the former European Agency for the Space Program (EUSPA).

The aim of this high level contest is to develop a location-based solution, such as mobile application, wearable-based solution, asset management, tracking solution, or robotics, leveraging Galileo as a source of positioning, navigation and/or timing.

V-Labs was selected out of 200 European leading start-ups. Many thanks to the jury and our team for making this all possible!
