Augmented Reality for Railway Contractors: A new tool to further optimize field work

High precision Augmented Reality allows VolkerRail to optimize fieldwork

In April 2022, V-Labs had the pleasure of presenting its high precision Augmented Reality solution for geospatial data to VolkerRail, one of the leading construction companies for the railway sector in the Netherlands.

VolkerRail uses geospatial data (GIS, BIM) and work instructions to guide people in the field through complex construction and maintenance projects. With new technologies, such as Augmented Reality, VolkerRail aims to further digitize its fieldwork, avoid shortage of qualified technical personnel and optimize its quality control.
